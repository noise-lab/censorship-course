  Worth a slide once the literature settles — currently only in speaker notes.

  Could connect to Ch. 4 economic controls.

  strengthen the "geography dominates" slide; flagged as the weakest dated figure.

  natural companion to the TikTok "For You" passive-signal slide.

  transparency / "addictive feed" laws would balance the regulatory picture.
